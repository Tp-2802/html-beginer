About F1
Formula 1 (F1) drivers are highly skilled athletes who compete in the pinnacle of open-wheel motorsport. They pilot high-performance racing cars, pushing the limits of speed and precision on renowned circuits worldwide. Key aspects include: multiple World Championship titles held by drivers like Michael Schumacher and Lewis Hamilton, the current four-time consecutive champion Max Verstappen, and the unique aspect of drivers choosing their own permanent car numbers (except for #1 reserved for the reigning champion).
